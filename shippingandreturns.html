Jewelry Care

Handmade jewelry can be delicate, so please wear with care. If handled roughly, crystals can break or chip. When bathing, it is suggested that you remove your jewelry, due to both water and chemicals from soaps and saltwater to break down crystals, and weaken other materials. Try to avoid getting chlorine, lotions, perfumes, and oils onto the jewelry. Place jewelry somewhere safe. To prevent tarnishing, keep your jewelry away from other metal jewelry and in a closed compartment/space.
